Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the base currency and/or conversion amount?

  1. If the currency you want to use is not on the main screen of the app, tap “+” and then select the currency from the Add Currency screen.
  2. From the main screen, tap on the currency.
  3. Type an amount on the keypad. You may also use the calculator functions.
  4. Tap “Convert” or “=” to set the base currency and conversion amount.

You may also use a “Quick Convert” amount by tapping any of the green buttons on the right side of the keypad.

Why are all currency conversions using the same currency symbol?

You have enabled the “Inverse Currency” setting, which you can disable within settings.

How do I remove the app ads?

You may upgrade the app from within settings to remove all advertisements.

How do I install the Currency app for Apple Watch?

Follow Apple’s instructions to use apps on your Apple Watch.

How do I use the Currency widget?

Follow Apple’s instructions to use widgets on your iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch. The widget is currently not available for Android.
